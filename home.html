ABOUT US

Your Trusted Landscaping Partner in Colorado

Barakah Landscaping has proudly served Colorado homeowners and businesses with a mission to make a meaningful difference in the landscaping industry. Originally established in Malaysia in 2006, our journey brought us to Colorado to bring our expertise and passion for outdoor spaces to a new environment.

We specialize in creating and maintaining beautiful, sustainable landscapes that enhance the natural beauty of your property. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing personalized service, attention to detail, and complete customer satisfaction.
